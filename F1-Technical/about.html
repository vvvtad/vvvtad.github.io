About Our Company

The Lunar Foundation (1990s)

Stellarium began as a private hilltop observatory built during the height of the space race. The founder’s grandfather originally funded the construction on a site chosen for its "unusually thin veil" between Earth and the atmosphere.


The Golden Era Expansion (2010)

As technology advanced, the facility transitioned from a simple telescope outpost to a public-facing planetarium. It was during this time that the iconic gold-accented architecture was established.


The Digital Renaissance (Present Day)

Today, Stellarium has moved its operations into the digital realm, allowing users to "sign up" for virtual voyages through the Milky Way.

About The Founder

The visionary behind Stellarium is a former astrophysicist who became disillusioned with looking at stars only through cold data on a screen.
He founded Stellarium to provide a "tactile" experience of the cosmos, believing that every person should feel the weight of the galaxy.
